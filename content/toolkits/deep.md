# Toolkit: Deep

**Focus:** Fill the calendar with high-value, complex cases.

**Promise:** Attract and convert patients with specific, chronic, or complex conditions who are actively searching for a specialist.

---

## Core Components

### 1. Condition-Specific Landing Pages
*   **Goal:** Create highly-targeted landing pages for specific conditions (e.g., "Sciatica Treatment in [City]", "Migraine Relief Specialist [City]").
*   **Content:** Each page should detail the condition, your clinic's approach to treatment, patient testimonials, and a clear call-to-action.
*   **SEO:** Optimized for relevant keywords to capture high-intent search traffic.

### 2. Educational Webinar or Seminar
*   **Goal:** Position the chiropractor as an expert and build trust with prospective patients.
*   **Topic:** A deep dive into a specific condition and treatment options.
*   **Format:** A live or pre-recorded online webinar, or an in-person seminar at the clinic.

### 3. Targeted Ad Campaigns
*   **Goal:** Drive traffic to the condition-specific landing pages.
*   **Platform:** Google Ads, targeting users searching for solutions to their specific conditions.
*   **Audience:** Highly-segmented based on keywords and location.

## Setup & Configuration
*   **Website:** Requires the ability to create multiple new landing pages.
*   **Webinar Platform:** (e.g., Zoom, Demio) if hosting online.
*   **Ad Account:** Google Ads account with conversion tracking set up.

## Metrics
*   **KPI:** Cost per lead (CPL); conversion rate from lead to new patient.
*   **Instrumentation:** Track form submissions on landing pages; use a unique phone number for each campaign.
