# Offer Structure

## 1. The "Win-Win-Win" Model
- Patient gets a discount on services.
- Partner receives a commission for each referred patient.
- Clinic gains new patients through partner referrals.

## 2. Standard Commission Tiers
- $50 commission per new patient exam.
- Additional tiers can be added based on volume or service type.

## 3. The "Golden Ticket" Concept
- A physical or digital voucher exclusive to the partner.
- Can be redeemed by patients for discounts or special offers.
- Helps track partner referrals and incentivize patients.

## 4. Terms and Conditions Stub
- Commissions are paid monthly based on verified patient visits.
- Discounts apply only to specified services.
- Partners must comply with clinic policies and ethical guidelines.
- The clinic reserves the right to modify terms with prior notice.
